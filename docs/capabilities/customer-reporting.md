# Customer Reporting

> Future scope: this capability is part of RelayHQ’s long-term vision and is not implemented yet.

## What it is

Customer reporting is the ability to produce work status updates for customers or external stakeholders.

Example:

- weekly progress summary
- milestone update
- delivery risk note
- completed work recap

## Why RelayHQ needs it

RelayHQ needs customer reporting because internal coordination is not enough once work has external commitments.

Reporting helps teams:

- communicate progress clearly
- reduce manual status writing
- keep external updates consistent
- connect actual work to customer-facing expectations

## Who uses it

- account managers
- project leads
- customer success teams
- delivery teams
- agents preparing status summaries

## How it fits the control-plane vision

Customer reporting extends the control plane outward.

RelayHQ should be able to take structured work data and turn it into:

- readable status updates
- progress summaries
- risk flags
- delivery narratives

## Boundaries / non-goals

Customer reporting is not:

- a CRM
- a support desk
- a full customer communications platform
- a generic document publishing system
