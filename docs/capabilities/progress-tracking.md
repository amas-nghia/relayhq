# Progress Tracking

> Future scope: this capability is part of RelayHQ’s long-term vision and is not implemented yet.

## What it is

Progress tracking is the ability to see how work is moving across tasks, plans, and projects.

Example:

- task states
- milestone completion
- blocked work
- ownership changes
- progress summaries over time

## Why RelayHQ needs it

RelayHQ needs progress tracking so teams can answer simple questions quickly:

- what is moving?
- what is stuck?
- what changed?
- what needs attention next?

It gives teams visibility without forcing them to manually reconstruct status.

## Who uses it

- team leads
- project owners
- operators
- agents reporting status

## How it fits the control-plane vision

Progress tracking is one of the main surfaces of the control plane.

It should help RelayHQ:

- show work state at a glance
- expose bottlenecks
- support human review
- create a reliable record of execution

## Boundaries / non-goals

Progress tracking is not:

- advanced analytics
- executive BI
- vanity metrics
- a replacement for status meetings in every case

## Future scope note

This is planned future scope and should stay focused on operational visibility and coordination.
